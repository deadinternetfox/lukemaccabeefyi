When working with serial devices, one of the most frustrating problems is determining the correct baud rate. Get it wrong, and you see garbled text. Many devices don't document their settings, or you might be reverse-engineering unknown hardware.

Baud Rate Brute solves this by automatically testing all common baud rates and detecting which one produces readable output - no more manual trial and error.
